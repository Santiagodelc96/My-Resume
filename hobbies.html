My Hobbies

Outside of my working hours, I seek to distract my mind doing what I like the most.

  • Motorcycling

    I'm passionate about motorcycles and especially and I love Royal Enfield bikes. In fact, I have a meteor 350 that has taken me everywhere in Colombia. Also, I really like organizing the activities of the motorcycle group that I lead, the Meteor Mavericks.

  • 3D Printing

    My love and affection for technology led me to buy a 3D printer. I like to print action figures, mechanisms, and things that are useful on a day-to-day basis and from time to time I like to customize the firmware of the printer, in order to improve its performance and capacity.

  • Video Editting

    I'm a very meticulous person with everything I do, especially when it comes to video editing. I use Adobe Premiere as my main editing tool and I am passionate about different filming techniques to give the content I create a professional touch
